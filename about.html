Who is it for?


Organisers

Organise, produce and promote your event. Openaire lets you collaborate with your colleagues, sends you alerts and organises all the information you need to make the event a success.

Sponsors

Using Openaire, companies can learn about the vents happening in their community and offer support to events that reach their target audiences. Offering a sponsorship or making a donation is easy to anyone using Openaire thanks to our payment integrations.

Attendees

Attendees registered in Openaire can receive alerts for events related to topics of their interest and buy discounted tickets for events. Furthermore, they can use the attendees functionalities while they attend their event: meet other attendees, check the schedule or comment and rate interventions, among others.

Presenters

Call for talks are now easier than ever - speakers can find gigs and see how the audience is ratinh their interventions...


How does it work?


+

Add your event to Openaire - just add a title, dates and address to get started.

&

Collaborate with your team in the different modules - from recruiting speakers to shopping for drinks or updating your stage schedule, you can find all you need to plan and organise your event in Openaire

$

Sell tickets for your event, accept payments and find sponsors to fund you event.

@

Enjoy your event! Openaire offers you ratings, social shares and an event wall; so that you and your attendees can get he most out of the event and share it with the world.
