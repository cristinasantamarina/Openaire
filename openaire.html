Openaire


Plan, produce, promote & enjoy events.

Openaire is an open application made with love for people who value goals over tools & like to keep things simple.

Start


Who is it for?

Organisers

Organise, produce and promote your event. Openaire lets you collaborate with your colleagues, reach your target audience, sell tickets and organise all your lists. All so that you can call your event a Success.

Sponsors

Learn about the events on your field and offer support to those that reach their target audiences. Offering a sponsorship or making a donation is easy to anyone using Openaire thanks to integrated payments and invoicing.

Attendees

Receive alerts for events related to topics of your interest and buy discounted tickets for events. Use Openaire while at events to see changes in the agenda, rate interventions and connect with other attendees.

Presenters

Are you a speaker? A panelist? A dj? Then you are the soul of the party; and you need a tool! Use your presenter profile to find calls for talks, submit your proposals and see how the audience is rating your interventions.

How does it work?


+

Add your event to Openaire - just add a title, dates and address to get started. If you choose to create a public event, we will add it to our database and tell potential attendees, presenters and sponsors about it.

&

Add collaborators to your event and add your management modules. Openaire lets you handle lists, finances, tickets, schedules... As complex or as simple as you want, and for as cheap as for free.

$

Sell tickets for your event and accept payments. Commissions? Yes. But we will help you make money before we take it off your hands: Openaire is the only event organisation platform that helps you find sponsors.

@

Enjoy your event! Openaire offers you ratings, social shares and an event wall; so that you and your attendees can get he most out of the event and share it with the world.
